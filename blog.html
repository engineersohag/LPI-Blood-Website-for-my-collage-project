"If there are two blood donors ready, The life of the pregnant mother will be guaranteed."

70% of pregnant mothers need blood during childbirth... and many pregnant women die from lack of necessary blood... So think of finding 2 blood donors in advance... Ask friends and known blood donors to always be ready so that they can be the first to come forward in case of any physical complications of the pregnant woman...

We have taken the initiative on behalf of www.lpiblood.com, Rakat Din, Jeeban Bachan and Call For Blood page... All our volunteers, well wishers and social workers come together...hope to help us in raising awareness about blood donation :)

Let's create awareness all over Bangladesh together... 64 districts everywhere... find blood donors for pregnant patients in advance - build this awareness all over Bangladesh...

" A request to popular personalities on Facebook "

There are some of our dear Facebook personalities who are very popular on Facebook through their writings... They have thousands of followers... I have a humble request to them today...

When I look at the home page on Facebook, I see blood requests for various patients... Little brothers and sisters are constantly working to find blood for the sick patient... The blood post means that 90% of people will pass it by, some will be upset; The remaining 10% people will see the request for blood... It is very difficult to get cure even from this 10% people... How much effort is required to find a bag of blood - we who are working with blood know very well, understand that...

Facebook popular brothers and sisters, you have a huge army, i mean followers... you have come forward thousands of times out of your sense of responsibility to collect blood for the dying patients... but also know that you can't post every blood request... even post every day. " Happy Blood Donateing Day "

June 14 is World Blood Donor Day. The purpose of this day is to encourage the general public to donate blood, including those who are saving millions of lives by donating blood voluntarily and for free.

World Blood Donor Day was first celebrated in 2004 following the experience of International Blood Donor Day since 1995 and World Health Day celebrated in 2000 with the theme 'Safe Blood'. Every year since the World Health Assembly in 2005, the World Health Organization has been urging to observe this day.

80 million units of blood are voluntarily donated every year, but only 38 percent of it is collected from developing countries, home to 82 percent of the world's population. Apart from this, still in many countries of the world, if people need blood, they have to rely on the blood donation of their family members or their friends, and in many countries, professional blood donors have been donating blood to patients in exchange for money. However, by collecting data from various countries of the world, it is known that the main basis of 'safe blood supply' is voluntary and free blood donation. Because their blood is relatively safe and the risk of life-threatening infections, such as HIV and hepatitis, in recipients is very low.

World Blood Donor Day on June 14 is dedicated to those hidden heroes who voluntarily and freely donate blood, to these unsung heroes. The celebration of 14th June has another significance. Scientist Carl Lundsteiner was born on this day. This Nobel laureate scientist discovered the blood group 'A, B, O, AB'. Donating blood is a great gift. Donating blood does not cause any harm to the body. In fact, blood cells are destroyed after a certain period of time.
So donate blood, save the patient's life.
