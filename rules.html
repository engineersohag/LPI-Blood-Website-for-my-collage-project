Rules

(Ensure your safety. www.LPIblood.com authority will not be responsible for any inconvenience.)

We are constantly struggling to collect blood for the dying patients. It is our responsibility to manage the blood of patients in an emergency, as well as to ensure the safety of a blood donor.

Telling the blood donors:

  1. Find out which hospital/clinic the patient is in. Do not donate blood anywhere other than hospital/clinic. Not even at the patient's home.
  2. Do not meet the blood applicant (the person you are contacting on the mobile number) anywhere other than the hospital/clinic. Do not ask to meet in the alley next to the hospital, or in a shop.
  3. Check the patient before donating blood. Check the patient's report, doctor's requisition letter.
  4. It is better to have two friends with you when donating blood.
  5. Make sure to use new needles when donating blood...
  6. If in doubt about the competence of the attending expert, inform the authority...
  7. Donate after blood cross matching, not before... most govt hospitals keep blood without cross matching... it should never be done... self aware... donate blood after cross matching...
Free blood donation

Blood donation is voluntary, free and safe!

If you want to go for blood donation, first talk to the relative of the patient... After knowing the name of the hospital, cabin/ward number, go directly to that cabin/ward... Blood applicant somewhere other than the hospital/clinic (the person you are contacting on the mobile number). Don't meet him... in the alley next to the hospital, or even ask him to meet him in a shop... not even at the patient's home...

Know the cabin/ward number, go directly to the cabin/ward... see the patient... Then donating blood... inform the patient and patient's relatives that you are donating blood for free... this will not give the hospital authority or any third party a chance to corrupt...Thank you!
