About us

Welcome to LPI Blood

Our work of LPI Blood is to serve the helpless people. We are bringing a huge opportunity to those who cannot arrange blood for their close relatives due to lack of blood donors. Users can easily find their nearest donor by using our website. We have here the list of all blood warriors of the country. Those who willingly donate their precious blood. We are here with 100+ Blood Donation Organizations. They always help people to donate blood using our website.

This website is created by us 4th phase 1st shift students of computer department of Lakshmipur Polytechnic Institute.

You can easily find your nearest blood donor by submitting the blood group, patient name, blood donation date, mobile number and where the blood is required. It is our responsibility to send the blood donor's name, mobile number, photo and address to you. which will reach the satellite within a few seconds.

Hopefully, no one from here will die empty-handed without a blood donor. Thank you!

Benefits of donating blood

Regular blood donation reduces the risk of heart disease and heart attack. Another study found that people who donate blood twice a year have a lower risk of developing cancer than others. In particular, the risk of lung, liver, colon, stomach and throat cancer has been observed to be much lower in regular blood donors.

First and foremost reason for donating blood: One person's donated blood will save another person's life.Blood donation is very beneficial for health. Questions and answers related to blood donation:

What is the correct age for blood donation?

The correct age of blood donation is 18 to 60 years old healthy healthy people can donate blood and their body weight should be more than 50 kg.

Is blood donation safe?

Yes of course blood donation is safe. Boys have 76 ml of blood per kg of body weight and girls have 66 ml of blood per kg of body weight. Voluntary blood donation involves collecting 380 to 400 milliliters of blood from a donor, which is 1/10 of the total blood in the body and half or less of the surplus blood.

Does blood donation have any side effects?

No, blood donation has no side effects. Rather, it has many benefits. Blood donation has many benefits for our body. As a result, the risk of bad diseases in our body is less.

How much blood is taken in blood donation?

About 380-400ml from your body. Blood is taken. Which is 1/10th of your body's blood volume. Because of this, most blood donors do not feel anything after donating blood and it is completely safe. The amount of fluid that is taken from the blood returns to normal within 48 hours.

After how many days blood can be donated?

You can donate blood after 3 months. But according to the World Health Organization, a healthy man can donate blood every 3 months and women every 4 months. But according to us everyone should donate blood every 4 months. If there is no special need.

How long does it take to donate blood?

Takes 5 to 7 minutes, maximum 15 minutes. Taking rest and other times may take 1 hour in total. By donating blood, everyone should look forward and serve people. Two lives can save you 1 hour.

Can I faint as a result of blood donation?

A very small number of people can lose consciousness if they donate blood. Since the blood collection is done by a doctor, there is no fear of getting sick. But after donating blood, rest must be done.

How is blood taken?

First a semi-series of blood is drawn from the left arm, for cross-matching and other tests. Blood is then drawn through a syringe in your right arm. There is slight pain when the needle is inserted. Then there will be no more pain. Your blood is easily collected through a tube into a saline-like bag.

What is the minimum weight for blood donation?

Although it depends on the height of the blood donor, the body weight of the blood donor should be minimum 47 kg for girls and 50 kg or more for boys. But weight should be at least 55 kg if platylat is applied, boy/girl.

Can smokers donate?

The lungs are particularly damaged by smoking nicotine, but a very small part of the consumed nicotine enters the blood, the amount of nicotine in the cells is in micrograms, but in the blood it is in nanograms, which is a thousand times less than the tissue, so the smoker can donate blood without hesitation.

Can diabetic patients donate blood?

No, Diabetic patients taking insulin should not donate blood. However, they can donate blood for special needs. However, if blood glucose remains normal through food control, blood can be donated

What should I do before donating blood?

Sleep well the night before. Have a good breakfast in the morning. Do not drink caffeinated drinks (tea, coffee). Do not eat too much fatty food. Drink enough water. Platelet donors should note whether they have taken aspirin within the last 2 days. It is better not to donate.

About to LPI

Lakshmipur Polytechnic Institute

Lakshmipur polytechnic institute was established in 2006. This institute conducts 4 years Diploma in Engineering course under Bangladesh technical education board.

In addition to technical education every technology has a Non technical education department for teaching compulsory subjects such as Bangla, English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Social Science etc to the students. Students are taught in two shifts Morning and Afternoon. Departments - Computer science technology, Civil technology, Electronics technology, Architecture technology, Electrical technology.

Lakshmipur polytechnic institute is located at Baishmara, 2 km east of lakshmipur district town. There are 23 labs with a five-storey administrative and an academic building, two storey workshops. There are also principal bus buildings, two storey staff quarters, a "Shaheed Minar", a sub-station and a pump house.

Every year after the release of the SSC exam results, Bangladesh directorate of technical education starts the online admission process in the Government polytechnics of the country. By filling the online admission form, students choose various departments and polytechnics. Departments and institutes are selected on the basis of merit and preference. Thus every year 600 students are admitted in Lakshmipur polytechnic institute in first and second shift according to the number of seats assigned in various departments.
